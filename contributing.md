# Contributing

Thanks for helping make Awesome Wallpaper Engine better! A few guidelines keep the list high quality.

## What belongs here

- Tools, libraries, integrations, documentation, and resources that are genuinely about [Wallpaper Engine](https://www.wallpaperengine.io/) (Steam app 431960).
- Open-source alternatives are welcome only in the **Open-source alternatives** section and must be clearly labeled as alternatives, not as Wallpaper Engine itself.
- Prefer actively maintained, broadly useful projects over abandoned or single-purpose ones.

## Submitting an entry

1. Add your entry to the most relevant section, in **alphabetical order** within that section.
2. Use the format: `- [Name](https://link) - Short description.`
3. Keep descriptions to one line, start with a capital letter, and end with a period.
4. **Verify the link resolves** before submitting. Dead or broken links are the fastest way to get a PR rejected.
5. Mark archived or unmaintained projects with `(archived)` or `(stale)` so readers know what they are getting.
6. Do not add your own project more than once, and only where it genuinely fits.

## Quality checklist

- [ ] The link works and points to the correct project.
- [ ] The entry is in the right section and alphabetically placed.
- [ ] The description is concise, accurate, and free of marketing language.
- [ ] No duplicate of an existing entry.

Run [awesome-lint](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome-lint) locally if you can: `npx awesome-lint`.
